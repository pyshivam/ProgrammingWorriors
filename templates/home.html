Programming Warriors by Foslipy Pvt Ltd.

PW is a not-for-profit educational initiative by FOSLIPY, an Indian Open Source company. We are creating a programming community that fosters learning and friendly competition. We are building a large community of problem curators.

At PW, we work hard to revive the geek in you by hosting a programming contest at the start of the month.

PW will help programmers make it big in the world of algorithms, computer programming and programming contests. We host featured contests every month and give away prizes and goodies to the winners as encouragement.

FAQ

  • Who can register for the competition?

    Only College can register and suggest 2 name (1 Group) of the students (Max 2 Group allowed from 1 college).

  • How Do I Send You All The Files?

    Upload your project to github and give us your project repository link.

  • What will college get?

    Winning college will get a Revolving Trophy (College name printed on the Trophy) and Letter of appreciation (in frame).

  • What will Students get?

    Winners will get a Prize Money, Certificate and a Medal.

  • Participation's Fees for joining the Competition?

    Competition Fees Per College is 1000/- Rs.

Rules & Regulations - Programming Warriors
  1. All programs must be created manually with understanding, no direct code copy & paste allowed.

  2. No team must interfere with the progress of other Teams.

  3. All information provided to register for Programming Warriors must be true and correct.
    You are solely responsible for keeping all information up-to-date.

  4. All submissions must be done on time, according to the given schedule.

  5. The decision of organisers will be final.
    In case of any queries, you can contact the technical/admin heads directly.

  6. You may not assign, delegate or transfer your registration for the Programming Warriors and/or any of
    your rights or obligations under these Terms and Conditions.
    Any attempted assignment, delegation or transfer shall be void.

  7. The winning institute/college has to organise the prize distribution ceremony in their campus.

  8. These terms cannot be modified or changed except with the written agreement of Foslipy Pvt Ltd.
